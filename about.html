About Us

Massage from Chairman

Since our launch in 2004, Arabian Gulf Investments has been able to gain the confidence of a large segment of investors and companies locally, regionally and internationally.

As a national company, we are committed to the directives of our Government leadership, we have worked to enhance the role of the Emirati citizen in our business and investments.

Arabian Gulf Investment Company contributes to supporting and encouraging various economic sectors, seizing investment opportunities and assisting local, regional and global companies and institutions, which drives economic growth.

We have been able to transform the challenges that the global economy has experienced into successful opportunities, as we have been able to take advantage of the current circumstances to expand, spread and develop our business significantly and remarkably to be among the most important UAE national companies at the regional and international levels.

Our main goal is to support the economy of our beloved country as well as expand in various countries of the world through strategic partnerships with our local and international partners in the real estate and investment field. We welcome more such partnerships that would support the business and investment sectors for the benefit of all parties.

As an Emirati national company, we have concluded successful deals that have effectively contributed to supporting the national economy and we are moving forward with steady steps in our business and investments to enhance confidence in the UAE economy. Our goal is to attract new investors looking for ideal opportunities that enable them to grow and develop in an active and safe business environment.

We will continue to provide our services with confidence and the highest level of professionalism as you entrusted us.

Khalifa Saif Al Muhairbi
Chairman
