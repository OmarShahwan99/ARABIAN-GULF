About Chaiman

H.E. Dr.Khalifa Saif Juma Saif Al Mehairbi

Found and Chaiman of Arabian Gulf Investment Group

Khalifa Saif Al Mehairbi is a young and ambitious Emirati citizen, whose personality combines the spirit of adventurous youth and the experience of experienced traders and investors. Khalifa is The Chairman of Arabian Gulf Investment Group and its subsidiaries, one of the fastest growing companies in the Gulf and North Africa region.

Al Mehairbi started his career by establishing his own company, which he named Arabian Gulf Investment Company in 2004.

Since established in 2004, AGI has been able to gain the trust of a large segment of investors and companies locally, regionally, and internationally. As a national Emirati company, Al Mehairbi worked under the directives of the leadership of the United Arab Emirates to enhance the role of the Emirati citizen in the company's business and investments.

Under the leadership of Khalifa Al Mehairbi and his team, Arabian Gulf Investment Company contributes to supporting and encouraging various economic sectors, seizing investment opportunities, and assisting local, regional, and international companies and institutions, which drives economic growth.

Khalifa Al Mehairbi, was able to transform the challenges experienced by the global economy into successful opportunities, as he was able to benefit from the current circumstances in the expansion and spread and develop the group's business significantly to become the Arabian Gulf Investment Group among the most important UAE national companies at the regional and international levels.

The main objective of the Chairman of the Board of Directors is to support the economy of the United Arab Emirates, in addition to expanding in various countries of the world through strategic partnerships with local and international partners in the field of real estate and investment.


Khalifa Al Mehairbi has achieved great success in concluding massive and successful deals that have effectively contributed to supporting the national economy, developing the company's business, and expanding in all investment fields.

Al Mehairbi directly supervises the deals concluded by the company at the international level and directly supervises the implementation of the company's strategy and the development of its business in various fields.

Arabian Gulf Investment carries out its diversified activities of investment in the real estate, health, and tourism sectors, as well as the acquisition of companies through its head office in the Emirate of Abu Dhabi or through its offices spread in countries of the world from China east to Europe and to Canada and the United States of America.

Khalifa Al Mehairbi has a high-level international network in most countries of the world, thanks to which he was able to contribute effectively to the completion of many acquisitions and strategic partnerships.

In Egypt, Khalifa Al-Mehairbi has a network of relations at a high level and works with credibility and high professionalism with a large segment of the most important investors, businessmen and the government, where the Arabian Gulf Investment Company recently acquired strategic shares in a group of Egyptian companies, including Easy Cash, the Egyptian Company for Embedded Systems and Industry, and Health Technology Company for Advanced Technology and Public Health, In the field of real estate development, Arabian Gulf Investment Company has acquired lands to establish investment development projects. Arabian Gulf Investment Company is currently working in partnership with the National Service Projects Authority to establish EGP 2 billion public investment company.

Through the company's branch located in Egypt, the branch, under the direct supervision of Khalifa Al-Mehairbi, invests in several major projects, including a luxury car rental company (24 Services), a luxury real estate rental company and the Arabian Gulf Company for Urban Development and Contracting.

The company owns many shares in a large group of Egyptian companies as an investor.

For more details about the Chairman and the company, please visit www.arabiangulfinvest.com
